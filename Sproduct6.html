    Epson Expression Home XP-4200 Wireless Color All-in-One Printer with Scan, Copy

    Npr-62999

    Product Details

    The Epson XP-4200 is a versatile inkjet printer designed for efficiency and convenience. With both Wi-Fi and USB connectivity options, it ensures seamless printing from various devices. This black printer features auto-duplex functionality and is Energy Star certified, emphasizing its commitment to sustainability. The model XP-4200 is known for its color printing capabilities with a maximum speed of 5 ppm and monochrome printing at 10 ppm. Weighing just 9.5 pounds, it combines a sleek design with lightweight portability, making it an ideal choice for users seeking a reliable and energy-efficient color printing solution.
